My all time favourite movies

RRR

A fearless warrior on a perilous mission comes face to face with a steely cop, serving British forces in this epic saga set in pre-independent India.

Bahubali

A child from the Mahishmati kingdom is raised by tribal people and one day learns about his royal heritage, his father's bravery in battle and a mission to overthrow the incumbent ruler.

KGF

In the 1970s, a gangster named Rocky goes undercover as a slave to assassinate, the owner of a notorious gold mine known as the Kolar Gold Fields.
